American magazine Variety has published the list of the 100 best TV series of all time.\u00a0This report is about the top 10 series of this list published last Wednesday.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1. i love lucy<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Topping the list is the famous sitcom series I Love Lucy aired on CBS Television in the United States. Lucille Ball played the title role in the series about the stardom of Lucy Ricardo, a young New York housewife. It also features actress Lucille’s husband, Desi Arnaz. The series ran for 6 seasons and 180 episodes from October 15, 1951 to May 6, 1957. The series is directed by Mark Daniels, William Asher and James Kern.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2. Mad Men<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Second on the list is Mad Men. The story of the series revolves around a famous American advertising company and its boss Donald Draper in the sixties. American actor John Hamm is known for playing this role. The series was created by Matthew Winer. The series aired for seven seasons from 2007 to 2015 on American TV channel AMC.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3. The Sopranos<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The crime and psychological genre series is based on the story of a mobster named Tony Soprano. It stars James Gandolfini in the lead role. The series, created by David Chase, aired on HBO from 1999 to 2007.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4. The Simpsons<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The animated sitcom series The Simpsons was created by Matt Groening. The culture and lifestyle of the country has come up in the series made about the members of the Simpson family. The series has been airing on Fox television since 1989.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5. breaking bad<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

American crime drama series Breaking Bad is in the fifth position of the list. The series, created by Vince Gilligan, aired on AMC Networks from 2008 to 2013. It stars Bryan Cranston, Anna Gunn and many others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

6. Sex and the City<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The series was created by Darren Starr about four women in New York. It stars Carrie Bradshaw, Charlotte York, Miranda Hobbs and Samantha Jones as the four main characters. The series aired on HBO from 1998 to 2004.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

7. The Wire<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The crime drama series is produced by David Simon. The series aired on HBO from 2002 to 2008. It stars Dominic West, Lance Radick, John Doman and many others in various roles.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

8. Seinfeld<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The sitcom series was created by two producers, Larry David and Jerry Seinfeld. The series aired on NBC from 1989 to 1998. It starred Jerry Seinfeld, Jason Alexander and many others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

9. The Mary Taylor Moore Show<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The sitcom series aired on CBS from 1970 to 1977. It was created by James L. Brooks and Alan Barnes.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

10. Roots<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Based on Alex Hale’s novel Roots: The Saga of an American Family, the TV series was produced by Marvin Chomsky, John Ehrman, David Greene, Gilbert Moses.
